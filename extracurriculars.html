Padua has tons of amazing extracurricular activities! From anime club, to cooking club, to theater, to drama club, and a cyber security team Padua has it all! Remember to not get involved in too many, though! It can be overwhelming when you have a meeting at flex for a club, but you also have to see a teacher. Join clubs that are fun to you so you don't dread going to them! But whatever club you join, try your absolute hardest to attend all meetings.
